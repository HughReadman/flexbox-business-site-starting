Behind the Brand

Meet the creatives, designers, and dreamers who bring Radge Supply Co to life.

Aylissa Banks - Design Lead

Aylissa crafts captivating designs with a keen eye for detail. With a passion for creative problem-solving and a background in graphic design, she brings innovative solutions to the table. When not designing, you can find her practicing yoga or indulging in true-crime podcasts.

Duke Halloway - CEO

Visionary leader and fashion industry expert with a passion for style and innovation. As CEO, he has driven growth and success through his keen eye for design and ability to stay ahead of the curve in the ever-changing fashion landscape. With a background in fashion entrepreneurship and technology, Duke has developed a unique understanding of what drives consumer behavior and what sets trends

Benjamin Sutton - Marketing Head

Results-driven marketing expert with a keen sense of style and a passion for innovative storytelling. As Marketing Head, Benjamin has developed and executed successful campaigns that drive brand awareness, customer engagement, and revenue growth. With a deep understanding of the fashion industry and its ever-changing landscape, he has a talent for identifying emerging trends and developing strategic marketing plans to capitalize on them.
